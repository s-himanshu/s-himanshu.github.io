Selecting the best Car For Your Road Trip

I do not own a car, and if you love road trips, this app is for You! This app allows to choose the best car for your road trip, based on some simple parameters. It should help you economise fuel consumption to save both money and fuel. This is especially important when your journey spans a few hundred miles or more. The basic parameters are:

  • Distance to Travel
  • Fuel Price
  • Fuel Budget

Advanced parameters available are:

  • Cylinders
  • Displacement Volume
  • Horse Power
  • Transmission Type

Source Data

MTCARS DATASET

The data used in the app comes from the Motor Trend Car Road Tests (mtcars) dataset. The data was extracted from the 1974 Motor Trend US magazine, and comprises fuel consumption and 10 aspects of automobile design and performance for 32 automobiles (1973-74 models).
